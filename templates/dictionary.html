A - D
Alpha
Exclusive information or early knowledge about potential profitable trades before they become public knowledge.
Example: "That Discord group shares good alpha on new meme coins."
Ape / Aping
Buying into a cryptocurrency without doing proper research, usually driven by FOMO or hype.
Example: "I aped into BONK when I saw it trending on Twitter."
ATH (All-Time High)
The highest price a cryptocurrency has ever reached in its trading history.
Example: "SOL just broke its previous ATH of $260."
Bag / Bagholder
A position in a cryptocurrency that has lost significant value. A bagholder is someone stuck with worthless tokens.
Example: "I'm bagholding this meme coin that dumped 90%."
Based
Expressing approval or agreement, often used to describe someone who speaks truth regardless of popular opinion.
Example: "That analysis about the market crash was based."
Bundle
A coordinated buying strategy where multiple wallets purchase the same token simultaneously to create artificial volume and price action.
Example: "That token launch was clearly bundled - 20 wallets bought at the same time."
CT (Crypto Twitter)
The cryptocurrency community on Twitter/X, where traders share opinions, alpha, and market analysis.
Example: "CT is bearish today after the Fed announcement."
Degen
Short for "degenerate gambler" - someone who makes high-risk trades, often in meme coins or extremely volatile assets.
Example: "Only true degens buy tokens with no utility for the memes."

Exit Liquidity
New buyers who provide liquidity for early investors to sell their positions at higher prices.
Example: "Don't become exit liquidity for those who bought the bottom."
Farming
Providing liquidity to earn rewards, or creating multiple wallets to qualify for airdrops.
Example: "I'm farming the new DeFi protocol for their upcoming airdrop."
Flip
Quick buying and selling for profit, usually referring to short-term trades or NFT trading.
Example: "I flipped that meme coin for a 3x in 2 hours."
FOMO (Fear of Missing Out)
The anxiety-driven urge to buy into a rising asset because you fear missing potential profits.
Example: "I FOMO'd into BONK at the top and lost 50%."
FUD (Fear, Uncertainty, Doubt)
Negative information or sentiment spread to influence market perception and drive prices down.
Example: "That article about Solana going to zero is just FUD."
Giga Chad
Someone who makes extremely successful or bold trading decisions, showing supreme confidence.
Example: "He bought the dip at $8 and held to $180, absolute giga chad."
HODL
"Hold On for Dear Life" - Long-term holding strategy regardless of short-term price movements.
Example: "I'm going to HODL my SOL through this bear market."
Jeet
Someone who sells too early or at the first sign of profit, often missing larger gains. Derogatory term for weak hands.
Example: "Stop being a jeet and hold for bigger gains."
KOL (Key Opinion Leader)
Influential figures in crypto with large followings who can impact market sentiment through their opinions.
Example: "That KOL's tweet about the project caused a 50% pump."
Liquidity
The ease with which an asset can be bought or sold without significantly affecting its price.
Example: "This meme coin has low liquidity, be careful with large orders."

When developers abandon a project and remove liquidity, causing the token price to crash to near zero.
Example: "The dev rugged the project overnight, taking all the liquidity."
