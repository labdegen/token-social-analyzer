🐕 MEME BIRTH
Dogecoin: The First Meme Coin
Billy Markus and Jackson Palmer create Dogecoin as a satirical response to crypto speculation, accidentally launching the meme coin revolution. ▼

Created on December 6, 2013, Dogecoin began as a joke but quickly attracted over 1 million visitors within 30 days. The project pioneered community-driven crypto culture, raising $50,000 for the Jamaican Bobsled Team and establishing the template for all future meme coins.

dogwifhat: The Hat Stays On
WIF becomes the first meme coin to cross $1, reaching $4.83 with a viral dog-with-hat meme and Ansem's advocacy, proving pure cultural appeal. ▼
